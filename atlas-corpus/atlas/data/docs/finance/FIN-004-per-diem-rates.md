# Atlas Industries — Per Diem Rates

**Policy ID:** FIN-PER-004
**Owner:** Finance Operations
**Effective Date:** 1 March 2025
**Version:** 2.3

---

## 1. What Is a Per Diem?

A per diem is a fixed daily allowance that covers an employee's personal meals and incidental expenses during business travel. Per diem amounts do not require itemized receipts.

## 2. Current Rates

| Travel Type | Per Diem | Currency |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic (within Egypt) | **500** | EGP |
| International (most destinations) | **75** | USD |
| International — Tier 1 cities (London, Geneva, Tokyo, Singapore, New York) | 110 | USD |

Tier 1 cities are reviewed and updated annually by Finance.

## 3. Eligibility

- Per diem starts on the day of departure (full day) and ends on the day of return (full day), regardless of departure or return time.
- An overnight stay is **not required** for per diem eligibility. Same-day business trips longer than 8 hours qualify for a half-day per diem (50% of the rate).

## 4. What the Per Diem Covers

The per diem covers:
- Breakfast, lunch, dinner
- Tips and small gratuities
- Bottled water and snacks
- Incidental personal expenses (laundry on long trips, etc.)

The per diem does **not** cover:
- Client meals (covered separately under FIN-CLI-009)
- Hotel costs (covered separately under FIN-TRV-001 / FIN-INTL-007)
- Ground transportation (covered separately under FIN-TRV-001)

## 5. Combining with Client Meals

If a meal is paid for by Atlas under the client dining policy (FIN-CLI-009), the per diem for that day is reduced by:
- 30% if a client breakfast occurred
- 40% if a client lunch occurred
- 50% if a client dinner occurred

The reduction is applied automatically by the expense system based on the receipts attached.

## 6. Currency & Conversion

- Domestic per diem is paid in EGP.
- International per diem is paid in EGP at the company's monthly published FX rate (visible in the expense portal).
- Receipts in foreign currency are converted using the rate on the date of the transaction (from the corporate card statement or the company FX rate, whichever is more conservative).

## 7. No Receipt Required

Per diem claims do not require itemized restaurant receipts. The only documentation needed is:
- Confirmation of travel dates (boarding passes or hotel invoice cover page)
- Manager-approved trip summary

## 8. Tax Treatment

Per diem amounts at or below the published rates are non-taxable to the employee under Atlas Industries' tax-residence framework. Amounts paid above the published rates require separate approval and become taxable income for the employee.
