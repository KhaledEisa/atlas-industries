# Atlas Industries — Travel Reimbursement Policy

**Policy ID:** FIN-TRV-001
**Owner:** Finance Operations
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Version:** 5.1

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines what business travel expenses Atlas Industries reimburses, the per-category limits, and the required documentation.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all employees on company-approved business travel within Egypt. International travel is governed by FIN-INTL-007.

## 3. Eligibility

- Travel must be pre-approved by your direct manager.
- For trips exceeding **5,000 EGP** in total estimated cost, a second-level approval (department head) is required before booking.
- Personal travel combined with business travel is not reimbursable for the personal portion.

## 4. Reimbursement Categories

### 4.1 Accommodation
- **Domestic hotel cap: 1,500 EGP per night** (room + tax).
- Higher rates require pre-approval from the department head and must be documented in the expense claim.
- Booking should be made through the company travel desk wherever possible.

### 4.2 Transportation
- **Taxis to/from airport: up to 800 EGP per leg**, original receipt required.
- Ride-hailing apps (Uber, Careem) are reimbursable. App receipts are accepted in lieu of paper receipts.
- Private car mileage is reimbursed at **2.50 EGP per kilometer**, with a logbook entry per trip.
- See also FIN-TRA-008 (Transportation Allowance) for non-trip-related transport.

### 4.3 Meals
- Personal meals during business travel: **per diem of 500 EGP per day** (no receipt required).
- Client meals: see FIN-CLI-009 (Client Dining policy) — separate caps and pre-approval rules apply.

### 4.4 Communication
- International roaming charges are reimbursed up to **300 EGP per trip** with itemized phone bill.

## 5. Documentation

Every claim must include:
- Original receipts (or app receipts) for all line items except per diem meals.
- A short trip summary: dates, destination, business purpose, attendees if applicable.
- The signed pre-approval email from your manager.

Claims missing any of these items are returned to the employee within 3 business days.

## 6. Submission Window

- Expense claims must be submitted within **30 days of the trip end date**.
- Claims submitted later than 30 days may be denied at Finance's discretion. Claims submitted later than 60 days are not eligible.

## 7. Processing Time

- Standard processing: **10 business days** from a complete submission to payout.
- Reimbursement is paid to the employee's salary bank account, marked as a separate transaction.

## 8. Compliance

- Falsifying receipts or expense amounts is a serious violation of HR-COC-004 and may result in termination plus recovery of paid amounts.
- Finance audits 10% of claims randomly each quarter.
