# Atlas Industries — Vacation & Annual Leave Policy

**Policy ID:** HR-VAC-001
**Owner:** People Operations
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Version:** 3.2

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the annual paid vacation entitlement for all full-time employees of Atlas Industries. It supersedes any prior vacation policy.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all full-time employees on a permanent contract. Part-time, contract, and intern entitlements are covered in policy HR-VAC-002 (not included here).

## 3. Annual Entitlement

| Tenure | Annual Vacation Days |
|---|---|
| New hire (year 1) | **15 days** |
| 2 to 4 years of service | 18 days |
| 5 to 9 years of service | 21 days |
| 10+ years of service | 25 days |

## 4. Accrual

- New hires accrue vacation at **1.25 days per completed month** of service, starting the month **after** the probation period (see HR-ONB-003) ends.
- Days accrue on the last calendar day of each month.
- Unused accrued days appear on the employee's HRIS dashboard within 3 business days of month-end.

## 5. Carryover

- A maximum of **5 unused days** may be carried over into the following calendar year.
- Carried-over days must be used by **31 March** of the following year, after which they expire.
- No cash-in-lieu is offered for expired days.

## 6. Booking Procedure

1. Submit a leave request through the HRIS at least **10 business days** before the intended start date for leave of 5+ days, or 3 business days for shorter leave.
2. Direct manager approval is mandatory. HR is informed automatically.
3. For peak periods (last week of December, Eid holidays), submit at least 30 days in advance.

## 7. Denial and Rescheduling

Managers may decline a leave request if it conflicts with critical business needs, but must propose an alternative window within 2 weeks of the original dates.
